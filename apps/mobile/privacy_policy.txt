Privacy Policy for Rentocostume

Last Updated: June 20, 2026

Rentocostume ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Rentocostume mobile application (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information with care. 

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App. Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.


1. Information We Collect

To provide our costume rental services, we collect certain information directly from you:

- Account and Profile Information: When you log in or register, we collect information such as your name, email address, phone number, and branch associations.
- Service and Booking Data: We collect information about orders, costume rentals, rental durations, payments, and branch preferences.
- Camera Access: The App requests permission to use your device's camera. This is used solely to scan item barcodes/QR codes and to capture images of costumes or receipts for order management.
- Storage Access (Photos/Media/Files): The App requests permission to read and write to your device's external storage to allow you to select and upload photos of costumes or documents from your gallery.
- Network & Internet Access: The App uses your internet connection to sync real-time order and inventory data with our servers.


2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following business purposes:

- Provide and Manage Services: To manage user accounts, coordinate bookings, track inventory status, handle pick-ups, and process returns.
- Customer Support: To contact you regarding order updates, payment issues, or support requests.
- Security & Prevention: To detect and prevent fraud, unauthorized access, or policy violations.


3. Sharing Your Information

We respect your privacy. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information with third parties. We only share information in the following limited circumstances:

- Service Providers: We may share data with secure third-party hosting partners (e.g., Supabase, Vercel) that perform infrastructure and storage services for us under strict confidentiality terms.
- Legal Obligations: We may disclose information if required to do so by law, regulation, or legal process.


4. Data Security

We implement standard administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect your personal data. However, please be aware that no security measures are perfect or impenetrable, and no method of data transmission can be guaranteed against any interception or other type of misuse.


5. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to provide our rental services and fulfill business purposes. If you wish to delete your account or have your personal information removed from our systems, you can request data deletion by contacting us at the support email provided below.


6. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.
